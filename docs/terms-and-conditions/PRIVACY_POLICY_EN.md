## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 Commitment to Privacy

At GSHOP, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our platform.

- The GSHOP mobile application (for buyers)
- The Seller Panel (for sellers)

This policy complies with:

- Applicable regulations from the Superintendency of Industry and Commerce (SIC)

## 3. Use of Information

### 3.1 Primary Purposes

We use your data to:

1. **Service provision:**
   - Process and deliver orders
   - Facilitate communication between buyers and sellers
   - Process payments and disbursements
   - Manage returns and refunds

2. **Account management:**
   - Create and maintain your account
   - Verify your identity (KYC for sellers)
   - Send notifications about your account

3. **Legal compliance:**
   - Issue electronic invoices
   - Report tax information to DIAN
   - Respond to authority requests

### 3.2 Secondary Purposes

With your authorization, we may use your data for:

1. **Marketing and communications:**
   - Send personalized offers and promotions
   - Inform about new products and features
   - Conduct satisfaction surveys

2. **Service improvement:**
   - Analyze usage patterns to improve the platform
   - Develop new features
   - Personalize your shopping experience

3. **Recommendation system:**
   - Suggest products based on your history
   - Show relevant content for you

### 3.3 Sensitive Data

GSHOP **does not request or process** sensitive data such as:

- Ethnic or racial origin
- Political or religious orientation
- Health data
- Sexual orientation
- Biometric data (except optional facial verification for KYC)

## 5. Data Protection

### 5.1 Security Measures

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

**Technical:**
- Data encryption in transit (TLS/SSL)
- Encryption of sensitive data at rest
- Two-factor authentication (optional)
- Access and anomaly monitoring
- Regular backups

**Organizational:**
- Role-based access restriction (need-to-know)
- Data protection training
- Information security policies
- Incident response procedures

### 5.2 Payment Security

- We do not store complete credit card numbers
- Payments are processed by Stripe under PCI-DSS standards
- We use tokenization for recurring transactions

### 5.3 Incident Notification

In case of a security breach affecting your data:

- We will notify you within legal timeframes
- We will inform the SIC as required
- We will take measures to mitigate the impact

## 7. Your Rights

### 7.1 ARCO Rights

According to Law 1581 of 2012, you have the right to:

| Right | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Access** | Know what data we have about you |
| **Rectification** | Correct inaccurate or incomplete data |
| **Cancellation** | Request deletion of your data |
| **Opposition** | Object to processing for certain purposes |

### 7.2 Additional Rights

You may also:

- **Request proof of authorization:** Obtain a copy of the consent granted
- **Revoke authorization:** Withdraw your consent at any time
- **Consult for free:** Access your data at no cost (maximum once per month)
- **File complaints:** With the SIC if you believe your rights were violated

### 7.3 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any right:

1. **By email:** Send request to legal@gshop.business
2. **Through the app/panel:** "Privacy" or "My Account" section

Your request must include:

- Full name
- Identification document
- Clear description of the request
- Contact information for response

**Response times:**

- Inquiries: 10 business days
- Complaints: 15 business days (extendable by 8 more if necessary)

## 8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

### 8.1 What Are Cookies

Cookies are small files stored on your device to remember preferences and improve your experience.

### 8.2 Types of Cookies We Use

| Type | Purpose | Duration |
|------|---------|----------|
| **Essential** | Basic functionality (session, cart) | Session |
| **Functional** | Preferences (language, settings) | 1 year |
| **Analytics** | Anonymous usage statistics | 2 years |
| **Marketing** | Personalized advertising (if authorized) | 1 year |

### 8.3 Cookie Control

You can control cookies:

- **In the app:** Settings > Privacy > Cookies
- **In your browser:** Browser privacy settings
- **Analytics opt-out:** Disable in account settings

**Note:** Disabling essential cookies may affect platform functionality.
