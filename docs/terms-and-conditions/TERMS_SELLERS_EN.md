| Category | Rate | Examples |
|----------|------|----------|

### 8.1 Consumer Right of Withdrawal

When a buyer requests a valid return:

- You must accept it if it meets legal conditions
- You must provide clear instructions for return shipping
- You must process the refund within 15 business days after receiving the product

### 8.3 Return Costs

In case of voluntary buyer withdrawal:

- The buyer assumes the cost of return shipping
- You must refund the product price and original shipping

- You assume all return and reshipment costs

### 8.4 Own Return Policy

You may offer a more favorable return policy than the legal minimum (for example, 30 days). This policy must be clear and visible in your listings.
