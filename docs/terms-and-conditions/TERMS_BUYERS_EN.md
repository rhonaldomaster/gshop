## 7. Right of Withdrawal and Returns

### 7.1 Right of Withdrawal (Law 1480 of 2011)

According to the Colombian Consumer Statute, you have the right to withdraw from your purchase within **five (5) business days** following product delivery, without needing to justify your decision and without any penalty.

### 7.2 Conditions for Withdrawal

To exercise the right of withdrawal:

- The product must be in its original packaging
- The product must not have been used
- All tags and accessories must be preserved
- You must present the invoice or proof of purchase

### 7.3 Exceptions to the Right of Withdrawal

The right of withdrawal does not apply to:

- Perishable products
- Customized or made-to-order products
- Personal hygiene products that have been opened
- Downloaded digital content
- Services that have already been rendered

### 7.4 Return Process

To initiate a return:

1. Access "My Orders" in the application
2. Select the order and product to return
3. Indicate the reason for the return
4. Follow the seller's instructions for return shipping

### 7.5 Refunds

Refunds will be processed within 15 business days following the seller's receipt of the returned product. The refund will be made through the same payment method used for the purchase.
